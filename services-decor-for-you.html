why decor for you

This unique home decor service offers inspiration and expert advice on decor for your home that will reflect your lifestyle and personal taste. Our decor experts are armed with advice on the 5 decor elements:

  • furniture
  • furnishings
  • accessories
  • lighting
  • colour combinations
  book an appointment
  • submit

risk-free experimentation

See your choices on a screen before they go on your walls.

insights into trends

Our experts are clued into the latest colour trends from all over the world.

at-home service

Get advice at home and at a time that's convenient for you.

in-depth consultation

Our consultant will take in all relevant details about you, your home, and your preferences before suggesting any option.

This service is present in the following cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, Bangalore, Chennai, Pune, Ahmedabad, and Hyderabad.

process

1st visit

Our décor expert will understand your requirements, lifestyle and room space. Additionally, colour combinations and textures will be finalised in this visit. Based on this visit, our décor expert will conduct comprehensive research and finalise 2-3 options on each decor element.

2nd visit

The consultant will present a customised décor kit comprising finalised options for each décor element along with pictures and purchase information.

The process of consultation will take around 1 hour for each room. Duration between the visits is a minimum of 2 days.

Wooden Surface

** PU sealer, matt and glossy has to be mixed with the hardener in the ratio 80:20 of base and hardener. The mixture is to be allowed to mature for a minimum period of 30 minutes and should be used within 4 hours of mixing.

++ Melamine sealer, matt and glossy - has to be mixed with the hardener in the ratio of 90:10 of base and hardener. The mixture is to be allowed to mature for a minimum period of 30 minutes and should be used within 8 hours of mixing. Melamine should not be exposed to ammonia vapour at the time of mixing or application / drying.

## Wood Stains can be used to give the required shade to the wooden surface. Wood stains can be mixed with Melamine, but not with TouchWood or PU wood sealer / finish. Wood stain is to be applied on raw wood before application of the clear coat. Or it is to be applied over the sealer coat and before the top coat. The wood stains are for interior usage only and will fade if applied on exterior wood. Normal Putty which is used to smoothen out walls cannot to be used on wood. The previous coat of finish has to be very thoroughly sanded for the new finish adhesion.
