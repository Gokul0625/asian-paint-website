4 pics

a painting colour preview service

Ever wondered how your walls would look with your favourite colour on them? Or which colour would complement the one that's on your mind?

Well, take a break and meet 4 Pics – a professional colour preview solution that lets you discover the perfect look for your interior and exterior walls, even before you paint it!

there are 3 different offerings that you can choose from

4 pics express

Mix n' match colours and create the look you want.

here's how:

  1. Simply upload a picture of your desired room and we'll email you the images in 'Pre-Cut Layers'.
  2. Upload those pictures on 'Screen-Test' and try out different colour combinations till you find what you like!

Price: ₹ 250/-

get started

4 pics print

Get our experts to send you a printed sample of how your walls would look with the colours of your choice.

here's how:

  1. Upload a picture of your desired room
  2. Choose your colours
  3. Hit submit!

Price: ₹ 500/-

get started

4 pics panoramic

Imagine looking at a 3D sample of your walls with your preferred set of colours!

here's how:

  1. Upload a panoramic picture of your desired room. *
  2. Choose your colours
  3. Hit submit!

Price: ₹ 750/-
